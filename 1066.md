## 00:00:00 Feature: Lou Reed
![lou](https://static.soundopinions.org/images/2026/813xori2n-l-sl1500.jpg)
Author Will Hermes is a longtime rock critic colleague of Jim and Greg. His 2023 biography, “Lou Reed’s New York,” was hailed for its fresh insight into one of the most analyzed musicians of the rock era.
