# Show 1048: Radiohead's Kid A 25th Anniversary Plus Malcolm McLaren



## 00:00:00 Intro: Coming Soon

Following up the guitar masterpiece of OK Computer, Radiohead threw the music world for a loop with Kid A. Twenty five years after its release, hosts Jim DeRogatis and Greg Kot talk with author Steven Hyden about how the album was made and its lasting impact. They also discuss the life and career of the Sex Pistols and New York Dolls manager Malcolm McLaren.
