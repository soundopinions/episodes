# Show 1064: Rapper Billy Woods, Opinions on Robyn & Courtney Barnett

![billy](https://static.soundopinions.org/images/2026/golliwog.jpg)

The prolific and singular rapper Billy Woods has spent decades carving out his own lane in hip-hop. This week, hosts Jim DeRogatis and Greg Kot chat with Billy Woods about his career, discography, and the process driving his music. The hosts also review new albums from Robyn and Courtney Barnett.

Jim and Greg review the first new album from Swedish pop star, {artist : Robyn}, in eight years. The album is called “{album : Sexistential}” and the hosts celebrate the honest and frank lyrics about aging and still wanting to dance.



## 00:00:00 Review: Courtney Barnett

![Courtney Barnett Creature of Habit](https://static.soundopinions.org/assets/1064/012.jpg)

Jim and Greg review the fourth album from Australian literate guitar hero, {artist : Courtney Barnett}. For her “{album : Creature of Habit}” album, Barnett has moved to Los Angeles and takes some inspiration from the Laurel Canyon sound.



## 00:00:00 Interview: Billy Woods

![billy](https://static.soundopinions.org/images/2026/golliwog.jpg)

Jim and Greg talk with enigmatic rapper {artist : Billy Woods}. Despite a string of artistic triumphs, Woods explains to the hosts that he still doesn't make enough money to support his family on his rap career.
