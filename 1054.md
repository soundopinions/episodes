# Show 1054: Radio DJ Mary Lucia & Opinions on Lucinda Williams
![mary](https://static.soundopinions.org/images/2026/mary.jpg)
Hosts Jim DeRogatis and Greg Kot talk with Twin Cities DIJ Mary Lucia about her memoir. They discuss interviewing artists, her eccentric family and her harrowing experience being stalked by a fan. They also review the new album from Lucinda Williams.
