# Show 1049: British Post-Punk Pioneers the Raincoats



## 00:00:00 Intro: Coming Soon

Hosts Jim DeRogatis and Greg Kot explore the music and legacy of the Raincoats, a post-punk band beloved by musicians and critics alike. Though they never found mainstream success, the Raincoats' sound left a deep mark on alternative music—including on one of their most famous fans, Kurt Cobain of Nirvana.
