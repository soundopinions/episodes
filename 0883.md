# Show 883: Jimmy Jam on Working with Janet Jackson, Prince & More

![jam](https://static.soundopinions.org/images/2022/jam-lewis-volume-one.png)

Since the 1980s, producers and songwriters Jimmy Jam and Terry Lewis have made huge hits with artists like Janet Jackson, Boyz II Men and Mariah Carey. But finally in 2021, the duo released their debut album as artists. Hosts Jim DeRogatis and Greg Kot chat with Jimmy Jam about the record and his illustrious past work.
