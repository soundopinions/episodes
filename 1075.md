# Show 1075: Drive-By Truckers' "Southern Rock Opera" 25th Anniversary



## 00:00:00 Intro: Coming Soon

Hosts Jim DeRogatis and Greg Kot revisit their classic album dissection of Drive-By Truckers' “Southern Rock Opera” for its 25th anniversary. They talk with co-lead vocalist and guitarist Patterson Hood about the album's creation and distribution, as well as how the themes resonate in today's political and social climate.
