# Show 1042: Fleetwood Mac's Rumours
![dont](https://static.soundopinions.org/images/2025/dont-stop-9781668054376-xlg.jpg)
Hosts Jim DeRogatis and Greg Kot talk with journalist and author Alan Light about his book “Don't Stop: Why We (Still) Love Fleetwood Mac's Rumours.” They discuss the music, history and why younger generations are still listening to this record almost 50 years after its release.
