# Show 1052: Phil Manzanera (Roxy Music) & RIP Bob Weir
![phil](https://static.soundopinions.org/images/2026/phil-manzanera.png)
This week, hosts Jim DeRogatis and Greg Kot talk with Phil Manzanera, the guitarist behind Roxy Music and a prolific record producer whose career spans more than five decades. The hosts also pay tribute to Bob Weir of Grateful Dead.
